Collaberating Towards a Brighter Future

ACL Global has been appointed one of the preferred distributors for Vodacom Financial Services. This collaboration is geared towards giving access to financial instruments which will enable enterprises and SMEs to grow sustainable businesses. By leveraging ACL’s distribution capability, Vodacom is able to accelerate its growth while creating a positive social impact through empowering businesses.

Partnership Services that we Offer for Vodacom are as follows:

Business Legal

Unexpected legal events can negatively impact the wellbeing of any business. With Business Legal cover you can focus on growing your business while the experts help with your complex legal matters.

Vodacom Ordering Solutions

Vodacom Ordering Solution (VOS) is an efficient order and supplier management platform that connects multiple buyers to multiple sellers on a secure digital platform. VOS improves the quality of orders received through an optimised ordering process.

Payment Services

Whether you are selling your products online, on social media, in-store or out of the boot of your car, we can help you accept payments safely and seamlessly – anywhere in the world

Business Lending

Whether you need funding to move into a bigger office, buy equipment or property or just to get cash flow, you need to act quickly and we can make it happen.

Group Funeral & Life Schemes

Ensure that your employees have the right funeral and life cover in place to financially support their families should the worst happen.

VotraTrade Portal

The VodaTrade Supplier Portal ensures that small suppliers are able to receive orders from their integrated partners quickly and securely on the innovative portal.
