Welcome To ACL GLOBAL

ACL Global, in partnership with Vodacom and Imbani Holdings, offers financial business solutions to firms and affiliates, including Online and Onsite Payments, Business Trade Supplier Portals, and Business Order Solutions.

The company thrives in driving disruptive innovation through focused market access insight, bespoke sales and distribution capabilities, tailored marketing as well as client-centric business process outsourcing. We deliver an integrated solution which focuses on creating customer value, brand positioning, product expansion, customer retention and improvement in channel economics.

ACL Global, a division of Imbani Holdings

ACL Global has joined forces with Imbani Holdings (Pty) Limited, the holding company for a collection of subsidiaries in high-growth areas in Southern Africa and throughout the world. Imbani provides a completely integrated solution from project inception to project completion, including project initiation and project financing expertise. This merger marks a key milestone in our journey to become the ideal partner for organizations seeking to establish sustainable enterprises both locally and worldwide.
